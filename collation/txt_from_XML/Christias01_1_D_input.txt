Mons ipse per umbras</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">horridior, magni clara argumenta doloris</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">pandit, olivifero laceros a culmine ramos</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">subiiciens oculis. Gemitus plangentis opacae</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">ingeminant valles et amici vallibus amnes.</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">Ipsi etiam aligeri, queis aethere dulce relicto</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">caelicolum domino dilectum invisere montem</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">et valles habitare sacras, purissima  tristi</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">gaudia praetexunt vultu, regisque supremos</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">(qua fas) sollicito miserantur amore labores.</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">Atque aliquis fortunati quem credita montis</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">cura premit, dulcique rapit custodia amore,</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">constitit et gemitu vix tandem in verba soluto:</p><p rendition="#rd-Text"> „Has” inquit „valles, hos seligis, optime, lucos</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">Leviadum in furias, periurique oscula Iudae?</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">Siccine Gethsemanes laudes nomenque decusque</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">infaustae noctis scelerisque infamia merget?</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">Spes mihi, spes superis fuerat, felicia tanto</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">hospite nocturnis domini celeberrima votis</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">auditura omnem  Iosaphati rura per orbem,</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">decessura tuo  Parnassi culmina monti,</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">Dictaeos saltus, Phrygiae sacra Dindyma matri, </p><p rendition="#rd-Text">Saturni Latium, vel si quid clarius illis</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">mendaci pietate alienoque auxit honore</p><p rendition="#rd-Text">in caelum tollens aevi longinqua vetustas.                                                                              </p></body></text></TEI>
